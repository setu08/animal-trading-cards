Asiatic Lion

Lions are often thought of as ferocious predators; the “King of the Jungle.”
They have a mysterious, majestic air to them that draws people in (usually to
see them at a zoo or, if you’re lucky, on a wildlife safari).

  • Sceintific Name: Panthera leo persica
  • Average Length: Male (1.7-2.5 m) & Female(1.4-1.8 m)
  • Average Lifespan: 10-14 years
  • Habitat: Gir National Park

Asiatic Lion is one of the seven sub-species of lions on this planet. Asiatic lions feed on wild pigs, cattle, antelope and deer in the wild. Present population of Asiatic Lion is 359 and is increasing every year.
